## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Cut down or quit smoking — on your terms, and in private.

Smoking Tracker is a calm, honest companion for anyone trying to smoke less or stop completely. No account required, no ads, and your data stays on your phone unless you choose to back it up.

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT
• Reduce OR quit — set a daily limit and cut down gradually, or aim for zero. Most apps only do "quit today." We meet you where you are.
• Honest streaks — one slip doesn't wipe your progress to zero. Momentum, not shame.
• See the real cost — every cigarette is priced from your brand, with today's spend and a 10-year projection that makes the cost impossible to ignore.
• Understand your habit — automatic insights on your triggers, peak times, and weekday vs weekend patterns.
• Private by design — works fully offline with no sign-in. Optional cloud backup syncs across your devices only if you turn it on.

WHAT YOU GET
• One-tap logging (long-press to add trigger, mood, or location)
• A daily target with an honest on-track / over-target calendar
• Health-recovery milestones with a live smoke-free timer (WHO/CDC based)
• A craving tool — a 10-minute delay plus a breathing exercise
• Gentle reminders and nudges that you fully control
• An optional companion you name and keep on track
• Money saved and progress you can actually feel

FREE TO USE
The core app is free, forever. An optional Premium adds cloud backup, deeper analytics, and guided quit-and-reduce programs.

Built for India first — rupee pricing, familiar brands, and local helplines — and useful anywhere.

Your habit is your business. We just help you understand it — and change it.
