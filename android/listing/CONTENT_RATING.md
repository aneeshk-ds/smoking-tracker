# Play Console — Content rating (IARC) questionnaire guidance

Answer honestly; the key app-specific item is the tobacco reference.

- App category: **Utility / Productivity / Health** (reference app, not a game).
- Violence: **No**.
- Sexual content / nudity: **No**.
- Profanity / crude humor: **No**.
- **Controlled substances — reference to tobacco: YES.** The app references tobacco
  and smoking, in the context of **tracking, reducing, and quitting** (a cessation /
  health tool). It does **not** depict use attractively, promote, or facilitate the
  purchase of tobacco.
- Gambling / simulated gambling: **No**.
- User-generated content / user-to-user communication: **No** (no social feed; the
  only sharing is a user exporting their own progress image).
- Shares user location: **No**.
- Digital purchases: **Yes** (optional Premium subscription) — declare in-app purchases.

Expected outcome: because of the tobacco references, expect a rating around
**Teen / PEGI 12–16 / ESRB Teen**. This is normal and acceptable for a cessation app.

Note: Google Play permits smoking-**cessation** apps. It prohibits apps that
**facilitate the sale of tobacco** — which this app does not do. Keep all copy framed
around quitting/health, never promotion.
