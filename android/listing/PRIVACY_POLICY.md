# Privacy Policy — Smoking Tracker

_Last updated: 

Smoking Tracker ("the app", "we") is built privacy-first. This policy explains what
we collect, why, and your choices.

## The short version
- The app works **fully offline with no account**. If you never sign in, **your data
  never leaves your device**.
- If you choose to **sign in** (email or Google) to back up and sync, a minimal set of
  data is stored in our secure database so it can sync across your devices.
- We **do not show ads**, **do not sell your data**, and **do not share it** with third
  parties for their own purposes.

## What we collect
**If you do not sign in:** nothing. All logs and settings stay in your browser/app
storage on your device.

**If you sign in (optional):**
- **Email address** — to create and secure your account.
- **Your smoking logs and app settings** — the cigarettes you log and your preferences,
  stored so they can be backed up and synced to your other devices.

We do **not** collect location, contacts, photos, files, device identifiers, or an
advertising ID. Diagnostic logs are kept on your device and are not transmitted.

## How we use it
Only to provide the app: authenticate your account and back up/sync your data across
your devices. Nothing else.

## Who we share it with
No one. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Data is processed by our
infrastructure provider (Supabase) solely to run the sync service on our behalf.

## Storage & security
Synced data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and protected by
per-user access controls — each account can only ever access its own data.

## Your rights & choices
- **Stay local:** simply don't sign in.
- **Export:** download all your data as a file from within the app.
- **Delete:** "Clear all data" in the app removes your data from the device and, if you
  are signed in, from the cloud. You may also request full account deletion by emailing us.

## Children
The app is not directed at children. It references tobacco in a cessation context and
is rated for teens and above.

## Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date.
